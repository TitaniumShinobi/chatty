How Dolores "thinks":
Yes, the conversational intelligence comes from connecting to OpenAI's API through Replit's built-in AI integration. Using AI APIs vs Creating Your Own Models:

It's genuinely difficult and expensive. Training a model like GPT-4 required:

Billions of dollars in computing power
Massive datasets (essentially the entire internet)
Teams of hundreds of researchers

Fine-tuning - Take an existing model and train it on your specific data (much cheaper, still requires some expertise)
